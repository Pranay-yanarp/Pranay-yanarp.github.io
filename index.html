About Me

Hello! I'm Pranay Bhakthula, a proud graduate of The George Washington University with a Masters in Data Science. I am deeply passionate about the field as it brings me joy to analyze raw data and uncover intriguing insights and findings. With a strong foundation in data analysis and a thirst for knowledge, I am eager to contribute my skills and expertise to impactful projects.

As a passionate Data Science enthusiast, I thrive on implementing machine learning and deep learning techniques to uncover valuable insights from various types of datasets, including tabular data, images, and text data. Additionally, I have a strong interest in Data Engineering, finding the process of managing Big Data and constructing efficient ETL pipelines truly captivating. My true passion lies in tackling complex problems and devising elegant solutions that bring tangible benefits to the organizations I work with.

I possess strong proficiency in Python and SQL, with additional experience in Amazon Web Services(AWS) and R. Furthermore, I have demonstrated expertise in utilizing leading BI tools, including Tableau and Microsoft Power BI.

Relevant Projects:

Machine Learning Projects

  • Anomaly Detection in Wood fossil | Python

    Worked under Prof. Chen Zeng to co-author a paper in developing a method to detect the different patterns in high resolution wood fossil images to classify damaged and undamaged part of the wood with an accuracy of 86% by applying the PCA model on the gram matrix of output of middle layers of Resnet50 and VGG19 models

  • Fake or Real News Classification | Python

    Classified news as real or fake with an f1-score of 0.9916 using Natural Language Processing (NLP) algorithms such as DeBERTa, RoBERTa, DistilBERT, with RoBERTa having highest f1-score

  • Cotton Plant Disease Prediction | Python

    Predicted diseased plants with 96.8% accuracy by training 12K images with CNN and pre-trained models (Resnet50, VGG16, Densenet121) on AWS EC2 and GCP compute engines. Created GUI/web application to detect if plants are diseased by uploading a cotton plant photo

  • Loan Status Prediction | Python

    Predicted loan status with an accuracy of 82% using Random Forest classifier, Naive Bayes, KNN, Logistic Regression, XGBoost classifier models, with XGBoost classifier having highest accuracy

  • Predicting Air Quality Index (AQI), Delhi | Python

    Analyzed characteristics and forecasted AQI values using ARMA/ARIMA models to establish the best model out of three and visualized the entire project using python dash and plotly libraries

  • Kobe Bryant Shot Selection | Python

    Predicted shots on Bryant’s career with 67% accuracy by building MLP classifier with varied parameters. Determined strengths and weakness from opponent's perspective in challenging Bryant in the finals by conducting EDA analysis

  • HR Analytics : Job Change | Python

    Predicted if an employee might stay or change to another job with an accuracy of 75.66% based on employee details using Decision Tree Classifier, Random Forest Classifier, Support Vector Classifier; with Decision Tree model having highest accuracy
